Descriptions of sessions

Jshroom Studio Classes

Introduction to Sewing

Come learn safty precautions to take when sewing. Then learn to sew a simple pattern with gathers, and straps that are turned right side out after you sew them.

Advanced Sewing Techniques

In this class you will learn out to prepare material to be cut, how to lay patterns out and cutting them out. You learn how to follow instructions provided with patterns. We'll teach you how to identify patterns that will fit, the amount of material to purchase and how to select the correct buttons, zippers and thread. It may should a bit difficult now but you will learn quickly.

Quilting Holiday Table Runners

There are are many techniques to quilting and as many different patterns and styles. We choose to do a table runner because it's one of the simplest forms of quilting as it doesn't require any actual inner layer nor does it require that you take the piece to a person with a long arm showing machine to create the actual quilting pattern on the top and bottom of it. This is a fun way to get started with quilting if you've always wanted to learn.

Intoduction to Resin

This introductory class will provide you with safety instructions, mixing and pouring techniques. You will learn how to layer resin and add embellishments, and how to use the uv curing light. The UV resin we will use is a quick drying resin and will allow you to take you pieces home on the day you make them. Where as slow drying resins take from 1 day to several weeks depending on the type and depth.

Create Jewlery with Resin

We'll use UV resin to make earing, or pendents you can embellish and layer for yourself or as a gift. This class is one of the most popular classes in the resin catagorie because it doesn't take much time to make a piece from start to finish. For all the resin classes you will want to wear old clothes that you won't care if they get messed up because resin can be a messy process.

Resin Coasters and Containers

Creating resin coasters and container can be quite fun. You will learn how to swirl colors and add photo to you piece. Remember its messy but fun.

Introduction to Crocheting

Learn about the different sizes of crochet threads and yearns, corchet hook sizes and terminology. You'll also learn how to master the chain stitch, half corchet stitch, single crochet, double crochet and how to use each one. We will not be learning patterns at this time but that will come soon enough and you'll be happily crocheting and amazing individuals who don't know how!

Intermediate Crocheting Techniques

Learn how to follow simple patterns using the stitches with yarn, you learned in the Into to Crocheting class. You'll also learn how to put different pieces together or make a hat or scarf. Once you start crocheting you may not want to stop!!

Crocheting Snowflakes

You'll learn how to tention your crochet thread and follow intricate patterns to make a beautiful snowflake you can starch and display. A bit of a warning crochet thread is a bit harder to work with so practice practice with your yarn.
